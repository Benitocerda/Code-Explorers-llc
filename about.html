Our Mission

To empower students—especially first-time coders—with practical STEM skills through projects that feel like play and build real confidence.

Our Approach

  • Standards-aligned: CSTA & AP CSP coherence.
  • Accessible: scaffolded pathways, multiple entry points.
  • Teacher-friendly: ready-to-run materials and PD.
